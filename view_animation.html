How It Works

This visualization shows a diffusion model generating text. Starting from complete noise (all [MASK] tokens), the model iteratively refines the text over 10 steps until coherent output emerges. Watch as the blue progress bar fills and masked tokens gradually reveal themselves!

Technical Details

Model: RoBERTa-base (125M parameters)

Training: 3 epochs on WikiText-2 dataset

Denoising Steps: 10 iterations

Schedule: Linear masking schedule

Sampling: Top-k sampling with temperature 0.8

Resolution: 1200x800 pixels per frame
